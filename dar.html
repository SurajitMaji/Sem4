"Queen Of Himalayas"

   The previous summer capital of India under the British Raj. Darjeeling has sought after hill stations in India. Located in West Bengal , this scence hill statoin is the perfect getaway for romantic honeymoon. Nestled amidst acres of tea plantation Darjeeling stands at the height of 2,050 meters above sea level, thus boasting a cool climate all year round.
    The third highest peak in the world and the highest in the India, the Kanchenjunga peak, is visible from here, and you can enjoy a panoramic view of peak. Some of Darjeeling's most popular attraction include monasteries, botanical garden. a Zoo and the Darjeeling-Rangeet valley passenger Ropeway cable car, which is longest Asian cable car. Tiger Hil is a fantastic spot to see the sunrise over the mountains in all its fiery glory.
